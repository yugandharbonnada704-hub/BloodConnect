Our Mission

Building a World Without Blood Shortage

BloodConnect was founded on a simple but powerful belief: no one should die because blood was unavailable. We bridge the gap between donors and recipients using technology, trust, and community.

Every 2 seconds, someone in India needs blood. A single donation — which takes less than 15 minutes — can save up to 3 lives. With BloodConnect, finding the right donor in an emergency takes less than 4 minutes on average.
